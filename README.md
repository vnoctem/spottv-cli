## SpotTV CLI
CLI tool to launch Spotify on turned off TVs connected to a Chromecast and control playback

##### To turn TV on
`spottv on`

##### To turn TV off
`spottv off`

##### To play a playlist defined in config.json
`spottv play [playlist_name]` (eg. `spottv play workout`)
